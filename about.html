History Of The Organization

Children with Dreams Foundation wants to encourage children's dreams. We are here to motivate them to achieve their dreams by exposing them to different opportunities. One that we teach is financial literacy development.

We work alongside communities to determine where their greatest needs lie. Our record of financial efficiency and transparency delivers the right support, in the right places, at the right time.
